# Intelligence Module

Gives hunters intelligence regarding certain things the Speedrunner does.

# Basic Events

## Boss Kills

Alerts whenever any boss enemy is killed.

## Shop Purchases

Alerts whenever the runner makes a purchase from an NPC shop UI.

## Toll Purchases

Alerts whenever the runner makes a purchase from an automated rosary machine.

## Simple Key Usages

Alerts whenever the runner uses a Simple Key.

## Lever Hits

Alerts whenever the runner hits a lever.

## Bell Shrines

Alerts whenever the runner rings one of Pharloom's great bells.

## Flea Rescues

Alerts whenever the runner rescues a Flea.

## Caravan Rides

Alerts whenever the runner disembarks from a ride with the traveling caravan.  Does not alert if the runner sends the caravan forward but chooses not to ride.

# Travel Events

Repeatable travel events have more complex notification settings, described below in order from least information to most.

-   Notify Local: Only notifies hunters of travel if at least one hunter is present in the room when the runner travels.
-   Notify Destination Local: Same as above, but the notification mentions the exact destination the runner traveled to.
-   Notify Global: Always notify hunters of travel, but do not mention the destination.
-   Notify Global Destination Local: Always notify hunters of travel, but only mention the exact destination if a hunter is present in the room.
-   Notify Destination Global: Always notify hunters of the exact destination of travel.

## Bellway Rides

Alerts whenever the runner rides the Bell Beast.

## Ventrica Rides

Alerts whenever the runner uses the Ventrica system in the Citadel.
